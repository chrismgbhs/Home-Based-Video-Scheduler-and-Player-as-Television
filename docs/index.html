WHAT IT DOES

Everything a real
station needs.

Built for anyone who wants to run their own TV channel at home — from movie nights to 24/7 personal broadcast loops.

📺
LIVE-POSITION PLAYBACK
Opens mid-show at the correct live position, just like tuning into a real TV channel. No rewinding, no waiting.
🗓️
SCHEDULE MANAGER
Schedule any video at any date and time. Conflict detection prevents overlaps — including commercials from adjacent shows.
📢
COMMERCIAL BREAKS
Assign ad breaks to shows at precise offsets. Visual timeline editor shows every break as a marker. Overlap prevention built in.
🔀
AUTO GAP FILLER
Empty time between shows is automatically filled with commercials. Weighted random selection and cooldown keeps it varied.
🖥️
BROADCAST OVERLAY
Now Playing, live clock, Up Next preview, and your channel logo — displayed over the fullscreen player with smooth animations.
📊
LIVE DASHBOARD
Full programme listing grouped by date, with ON AIR NOW highlighting and commercial sub-rows under each show. Updates live.

QUICK START

Up and running
in five steps.

01
ADD YOUR VIDEOS
Open the Video Library and click + Add Files or + Add Folder. Durations are read automatically in the background using a shared LibVLC instance.
02
BUILD YOUR SCHEDULE
Open the Schedule Manager, pick a video, set a start date and time, and click Add. The next suggested time auto-fills after each entry — always based on True End Time.
03
ADD COMMERCIALS (OPTIONAL)
Add ad clips to the Commercial Library, assign weights to control play frequency, then assign them to specific shows at specific offsets in the visual timeline editor.
04
CONFIGURE YOUR STATION
Upload your channel logo in Settings, adjust its size (40–200px) and opacity (10–100%), and toggle the overlay and auto-fill commercials on or off.
05
GO ON AIR
Click Video Player on the main menu. The player opens fullscreen, seeks to the correct live position, and your station is live.

UNDER THE HOOD

Built on solid

PLAYBACK ENGINE
LibVLC renders into a native Win32 HWND. A separate transparent overlay window uses WS_EX_TRANSPARENT to draw on top — the only reliable way to layer WPF content over VLC.
TRUE END TIME
Every show has a True End Time = video end + all commercial durations. Conflict detection uses this everywhere — you can never schedule into a commercial break.
GAP FILLER
Uses a wall-clock DispatcherTimer — not MediaPlayer.Time — so commercial cuts happen at exact wall-clock times with zero drift.
